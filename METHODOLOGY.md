# Methodology

## Data Source

Microdata from the Brazilian Higher Education Census (INEP).

## Temporal Coverage

1995 – Latest available year.

## Inclusion Criteria

Programs whose official denomination contains the expression:
"Inteligência Artificial".

## Exclusion Criteria

Programs without explicit textual denomination.

## Analytical Procedures

- Historical reconstruction
- Academic level separation
- Annual frequency computation
- Institutional counting
- Time-series visualization
